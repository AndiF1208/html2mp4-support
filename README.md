# HTML2MP4 — Support

HTML2MP4 is a macOS app that turns HTML & CSS into an MP4 video, entirely on your Mac. Your files never leave your computer.

## Frequently asked questions

**How do I convert HTML to video?**
Open the app, paste or load your HTML/CSS, then click **Generate Video**. When rendering finishes, click the **download** button to save the MP4.

**Where is the video saved?**
You choose the location in the standard macOS save dialog (it defaults to your Downloads folder). HTML2MP4 only writes to the location you pick.

**Does it need an internet connection?**
No. All rendering and video encoding run locally on your Mac. The app makes no network calls to convert your content.

**Which output format do I get?**
MP4 where your Mac's encoder supports it, with an automatic WebM fallback on older systems.

**What are the system requirements?**
macOS 12 (Monterey) or later, Apple Silicon.

**Is there a subscription or account?**
No. HTML2MP4 has no account, no subscription, and no in-app purchases.
